Founded by a team of FinTech veterans, QuestDB were part of Y Combinator’s S20 batch. Their product: An open source database designed to make time-series lightning fast and easy. That makes QuestDB very well suited for financial market data.

All they needed now was a website that reflected their ingenious creation. That’s where I came in… to help manage the project delivery.
We made it our priority to clarify and simplify the web experience — making it actionable especially for QuestDB’s primary audience, developers. With navigable documentation, interactive code blocks and a squeaky clean tutorial hub. It even has a day & night mode… what more could you want as a developer?
Today QuestDB has been Starred on GitHub more that 10,000 times and is being used by finance industry leaders such as Yahoo Finance, OKX & Central Group. They also raised a $12M Series A, backed by leading enterprise VCs like Seedcamp.
