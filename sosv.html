SOSV is a venture capital fund with a taste for plant-based; writing checks for founder with sustainable ideas. Cell-grown meatballs… Molecular whiskey… Ice-cream without the cream, just a few of the things they’ve funded and taken to market.

They also help founders with product, community building, and GTM strategy; which is how we got talking. Back in September 2020, SOSV launched Momentum; a pre-accelerator program specifically for PhD candidates. Sava & Hugh were looking for some assistance in getting the word out there. The challenge was that COVID restrictions mean’t SOSV didn’t have an active presence on university campuses. We had to get creative.
For the Momentum team a promo-video was the answer. Together we carefully planned what this video should achieve; communicating exactly what Momentum did and ultimately achieving sign-ups.

It needed to be exciting, yet informative and concise; featuring success stories of previously accelerated startups and a few words from the general partners.

Our finished video was well-received by universities with SOSV securing partnership with Imperial College London. The Momentum program was subsequently rolled out the program by November and quickly generated sigh-ups.
