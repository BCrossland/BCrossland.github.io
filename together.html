Together is a full-service agency made up of super talented designers, writers & engineers working with ambitious tech companies from Stockholm to San Fran. I joined the Together team at an exciting time. While the world was still in lockdown, Together was just taking off with a client base rapidly shifting beyond pre-seed and series A startups to well funded Series B & C stage companies.

Fresh out of university it was my job to lead a small development team delivering projects with Coast, Skupos & Hive Streaming and implement marketing channel initiatives. The deadlines were tight and there was limited budget.
To increase project turnaround time we introduced some automated tools, including Monday.com, that allowed both developers and the client to better track progress.
Where possible I outsourced a number of technical requests that had been taking away our focus from product development. For instance, Hive Streaming wanted branded templates in PowerPoint & Word that could be easily edited by non-technical staff members. There are people on Fiverr that specialise in exactly this!

To expand our social media presence I focused heavily on LinkedIn, a platform that had major organic growth potential. The messaging was engaging, demonstrated our creative abilities and gave an insight into the company culture.

Our clients were happy, we knew that because they told us so on Clutch ↗. Developers were also happy! They could remain focussed on building the product, safe in the knowledge that deadlines were being met on time and according to specification. In terms of social media, within only three months our LinkedIn following had grown 500% — and we didn’t spend anything.
