Founded in 2001, DONT WALK Charity Fashion Show has become the largest student fashion show in the United Kingdom. I began working with them back in 2018 to assist with branding and communication, so I was thrilled to get the call to consult on web development for the 2022 show. Over a 20-year history DONT WALK has always been dark and provocative but that wasn't being communicated on the website.

The existing website utilised no-code solutions that didn’t have the user experience worthy of the brand. Navigation was unresponsive and confusing. It was time for a fresh look.
The first step was to align with the marketing director, Allegra Tenanbaum. Going back to the brand’s dark core yet keeping things clean and simple, stripping back a lot of the necessary content from the old website.

Referring to Google Analytics data from previous websites, the models and committee have always been the point of interest for visitors. We brought them back into focus. We also wanted to ensure that the 2022 sponsors — Coca-Cola, Morgan Stanley, Heineken — were well represented.

The 2022 show marked the first major event since the pandemic — and it exceeded expectation. From a communications and PR perspective, DONT WALK reached new heights landing notable features in international publications such as Vogue and CR Fashion Book. Most of all I’m proud that we created a website that could easily be kept in place over the next few years. The average session per visitor on the website went up by minutes and we consistently ranked within the top 3 results on Google.
